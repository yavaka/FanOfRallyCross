JOHAN KRISTOFFERSSON

A news headline of the 2019-2020 off-season, was double FIA World Rallycross Champion Johan Kristoffersson returning to the series after a gap-year driving a Volkswagen Golf in the WTCR for Sebastien Loeb Racing. The most successful driver of the Championship will return as a single car entry in a family run team, but at the wheel of the Volkswagen Polo used in his dominating 2017 and 2018 campaigns.

Following a handful of European RX and World RX outings in 2013 and 2014, Kristoffersson made the full-time switch to the FIA World Rallycross Championship in 2015 with Volkswagen Team Sweden, winning the curtain raising World RX of Portugal event and finishing on the podium a further four times, going on to claim third in the overall standings.

A second career success followed at World RX of France in 2016 en route to clinching the runner-up spot behind countryman Mattias Ekstrom, a breakthrough season that would ultimately set him up for his dominant title-winning World RX campaign in 2017.

Paired with Petter Solberg in the rebranded PSRX Volkswagen Sweden team and driving a factory-developed Volkswagen Polo Supercar, Kristoffersson swept to seven victories – including five-in-a-row – to win the 2017 drivers’ title by a sizeable margin. Johan proved all-conquering in 2018 with 11 wins out of 12 on his way to his second World Championship crown.
