ROBIN LARSSON

Double FIA European Rallycross Champion for Supercars and WorldRX event winner in Argentina 2015, Robin Larsson will bring his flamboyant driving style back to the World Championship after dominating the European scene in 2019. In his first year at the wheel of the EKS-built Audi S1, Larsson won the first four events of the five-round European Championship to secure the title with the JC Raceteknik team. This year will see Robin run the latest version of the car, which narrowly lost out to Timmy Hansen in the hands of Andreas Bakkerud. Robin will strengthen the Swedish contingent of the FIA World Rallycross Championship, with five drivers taking part in the full campaign.
